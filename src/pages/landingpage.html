KOFI - About Us

Kofi was founded by Mohammad Awada and Christopher Yammine in 2023. With its thrilling atmosphere and unique menu, Kofi remains one of the top coffeeshops in Beirut with 8 locations and over 100 employees. Kofi aims to become the getaway of all people all around Lebanon since Kofi is a place that is open for diversity and inclusion.

KOFI - Our Mission

Our Mission is to create and enhance a space for lebanese people who cannot afford a rental office space or are suffering with connection issues (because you know,Lebanon). Kofi has also established a monthly membership for those who are willing to join our family and spend their entire day with us. In Conclusion, it was a cup of coffee that combined chris with mohammad, so their mission is to connect the whole country with a cup of coffee made with beans of robust and love.

KOFI - Our Products

Our Products are made with 100% real authentic coffee from various countries such as Ethiopia, Brazil, Columbia, and local lebanese coffee. Kofi was able to merge coffee types and coffee procedures of making (acidic, basic, neutral) that resulted in a unique taste that everyone fall in love with. In addition to cup of coffees, Kofi also provides wholesale coffee beans and instants for those who like to experience the phenomenal taste whenever they want wherever they want.
Kofi's signature product is the Kofuccino, an authentic cappuccino made from Dominican coffee with high quality milk from the Netherlands.
Kofi also has many products that made more than 30,000 customer in love with us, such as American coffee, french burner, caffe latte, and our famous Ethiopian Robusto espresso.
As of June 2023, Kofi now has online delivery all around Beirut and its suburbs, enabling people to experience the outsanding experience of Kofi One.Click.Away.
